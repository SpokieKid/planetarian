产品介绍

X is a narrative-driven game where you get to experience the evolution of mankind from the international world to a Planetary world, in which the society is greatly supported by blockchain technologies, pioneered by Base.

You will be put into 3 different worlds, and you will get to simulate a life in the world, and feel the differences of 3 worlds.

The game aims to be lightweight and educational, filled with fun network narratives, to show the Base’s capabilities of upholding the Planetary Onchain Economy.

产品详情

用户流程

UI

  • 类似 Sims ，纪元 1800 （Anno 1800）这种俯视角的模拟经营类游戏

剧情

  • 剧情风格参考用户流程中的流程图，对于一个事件有多个解决和故事节点
  • 类似 Galgame 的风格类型，打造出网状叙事的结构

技术架构

  • 打造出网状叙事的框架，让之后剧情设计师可以方便地对游戏剧情进行增删改
